This composite mission combines two overlapping drone missions flown at different altitudes:

- [Mission 001406](/data/drone/mission-details/001406/) (higher altitude, nadir camera pitch)
- [Mission 001407](/data/drone/mission-details/001407/) (lower altitude, oblique camera pitch)

## Raw drone images

Access the raw drone images via the constituent missions:

- [Mission 001406](/data/drone/mission-details/001406/) (higher altitude, nadir camera pitch)
- [Mission 001407](/data/drone/mission-details/001407/) (lower altitude, oblique camera pitch)
